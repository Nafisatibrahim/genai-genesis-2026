# FlexCare — Safety disclaimer (source of truth)

Use this text in the app UI (e.g. footer or first screen) and in any public-facing docs.

---

**FlexCare is for education and care navigation only.** It does not diagnose conditions, replace a healthcare professional, or provide medical advice. Always seek professional care for persistent pain, new or worsening symptoms, or any red flags (e.g. numbness, loss of bladder/bowel control, chest pain, trauma). When in doubt, see a doctor or other qualified provider.
